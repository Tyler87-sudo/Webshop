Pedals

MXR EVH 5150 Overdrive

The MXR EVH 5150 Overdrive is a powerful pedal designed in collaboration with Eddie Van Halen. It offers high-gain, tube-like saturation with controls for output, gain, bass, mid, treble, and a boost switch.


Ibanez Tube Screamer Mini

The Ibanez Tube Screamer Mini is a compact overdrive pedal delivering the classic Tube Screamer tone. With simple controls for overdrive, tone, and level, it's perfect for adding warm, vintage drive to any setup.


Dunlop Cry Baby GCB95

The Dunlop Crybaby GCB95 is a legendary wah pedal known for its expressive and iconic wah-wah sound. It features a rugged construction and a straightforward design, making it a staple for guitarists of all genres.
